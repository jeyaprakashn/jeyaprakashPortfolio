About Me

I am Jeyaprakash Naganathapandian, Co-Founder and Director of ATC Technologies, a software development and technical training company based in Chennai. With extensive experience as a Trainer, Technical Content Developer, and Edu-Tech Startup Owner, I design and deliver industry-focused training programs for engineering and arts colleges.

I specialize in Full Stack Development, Java, C, C++, Python, Embedded Systems, IoT, Robotics, React Native, and Computer Science Fundamentals. I also conduct workshops such as Code to Career, IT placement guidance, and faculty development programs on languages like Kotlin, Dart, and Go.

As part of ATC Technologies, I collaborate with colleges to provide impactful training programs, hands-on bootcamps, and customized academic support for departments like CSE, IT, and ECE. I also mentor students on projects, placements, and modern engineering skills.
